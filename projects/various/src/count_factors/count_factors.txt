Count Factors of a Number
-------------------------

Write a program that counts the factors of an integer number.


Test examples:
   6             - >  4      because: 1, 2, 3, 6
   15            ->   4      because: 1, 3, 5, 15
   16            ->   5      because: 1, 2, 4, 8, 16
   24            ->   8      because: 1, 2, 3, 4, 6, 8, 12, 24
   512           ->  10
   12400         ->  30
   1_000_000     ->  49
   20_147_483    ->   2
   5_042_483_640 ->  96
